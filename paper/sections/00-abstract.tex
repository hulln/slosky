This paper presents the construction and analysis of a corpus of 141,013 public Slovene posts by 432 authors on Bluesky, spanning August 2023 to April 2026. The corpus was built through a multi-stage ATProto pipeline: network-wide discovery of Slovene-tagged posts via the public relay and firehose, full-history backfill of all discovered authors from their home Personal Data Servers, and conservative post-level language filtering using langid and langdetect. Manual validation confirmed near-perfect precision for the included decision groups. The resulting corpus captures a community in formation --- a sharp late-2024 growth surge is visible in the corpus, 68.3\% of posts are replies rather than original content, and a small core of 22 authors accounts for half the corpus. The paper documents the collection workflow, validates its reliability, and presents first observations on the interactional structure, temporal patterns, and multilingual character of Slovene Bluesky use.
