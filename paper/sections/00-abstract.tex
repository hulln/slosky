This paper presents a corpus of 141,013 public Slovene posts by 432 authors on Bluesky, spanning August 2023 to mid-April 2026. The corpus was built through a multi-stage ATProto pipeline: relay-based discovery of Slovene-tagged posts, backfill of surviving public post histories from authors' home Personal Data Servers, and post-level language filtering using langid and langdetect. Manual validation indicates high precision for the included decision groups. The resulting corpus captures a community in formation: a sharp late-2024 growth surge, 68.3\% of posts being replies rather than original content, and a small core of 22 authors accounting for half the corpus. The paper documents the collection workflow, reports on its validation, and describes the basic interactional and temporal patterns of Slovene Bluesky use. It also discusses the legal and ethical questions that accompany corpus construction on decentralised, open-protocol platforms.
